HOW CAN I BUY THE MSMECOIN?

THERE IS APPROXIMATELY FOUR STEPS TO ACQUIRING MSMECOIN

STEP 1: CREATE A WALLET

Download trustwallet and create a wallet. Keep your phrase a secret, never share it with anyone and ensure to store your seed phrase safely.

STEP 2: ADD MSMECOIN TO YOUR TRUST WALLET

Copy the MSMECOIN Contract address here:

0xf575a31d804b2034cab5f96d4db500907e720d3b

Tap the icon in the top-right and search for “MSMECOIN”. If you cant find it, click on “ADD CUSTOM TOKEN”.

At the right side of your wallet, tap “Ethereum” next to Network, and change it to “Smart Chain”. Copy the MSMECOIN contract address above and paste it in the wallet contract address box.

Next, put “MSMECOIN” as the name, and MSME as the symbol. Decimal will be 18. Click “Done” at the top and you should now have MSMECOIN added to your wallet, BRAVO!

STEP 3: BUY “BINANCE SMART CHAIN” (BSC)

Tap on “Smart Chain” on the main screen of trust wallet, then tap “Buy” in the top right. This step may require KYC verification to prove your identity, so have your valid documents handy. After purchasing your BSC, wait for transaction to be processed. Once your transaction is completed, you are ready to buy some MSMECOIN!

STEP 4: SWAP BSC FOR MSMECOIN

Open the DAPP or Browser section and find PancakeSwap and open it. Connect your trust wallet at the top-right, scroll down a little to the “EXCHANGE” icon click and set spillage to 11%. For fast transaction, increase your deadline and by default, it should be set to 20 minutes and your are ready to hold some MSMECOIN!.

For iPhone users, go to browser at the bottom of the main screen. If the Browser button is not visible at the bottom for iPhone, open and click on the url to type trust://brower_enable, then return trust wallet.
